# VanRakshak-X: Cinematic "War Room" Demo Script

Follow this script to deliver a high-impact, emotional systems-storytelling demo.

## Phase 1: The Baseline (0:00 - 0:30)
*   **Action**: Show the Dashboard Home.
*   **Narrative**: "Welcome to the VanRakshak Eco-Command Center. Right now, our forest is in a steady state. You can see our **Bioacoustic Stability Index (BSI)** is at 92%—birds are active, and the ecosystem is breathing."
*   **Visual**: Point to the BSI chart and the "System Online" indicator.

## Phase 2: The Precursor (0:30 - 0:50)
*   **Action**: Simulate a "Silence Anomaly" (Manual trigger or code change).
*   **Narrative**: "Suddenly, we detect an anomaly. Before a single chainsaw starts, the BSI drops to 40%. The wildlife has gone quiet. The system has detected human intrusion through ecological silence."
*   **Visual**: Dashboard BSI bar drops; status changes to 'SUSPICIOUS'.

## Phase 3: The Threat (0:50 - 1:10)
*   **Action**: Play a chainsaw sound or trigger a high-confidence audio alert.
*   **Narrative**: "Now, the acoustic signature confirms it: a 1D-CNN on our ESP32-S3 node has identified a chainsaw with 94% confidence. Simultaneously, our **Smart Tree Digital Twin** detects mechanical stress through trunk vibration."
*   **Visual**: Dashboard pulses red; 'CHAINSAW DETECTED' alert appears.

## Phase 4: The Localization (1:10 - 1:30)
*   **Action**: Show the Red Pulse on the Map.
*   **Narrative**: "We don't just know there's a threat; we know exactly where it is. Using **TDOA Triangulation**, we've localized the source to Zone 4B, with a precision of ±4.5 meters. A ranger is automatically notified via Telegram."
*   **Visual**: Point to the Mapbox localization pulse and the 'Localized' badge.

## Phase 5: The Impact (1:30 - 1:45)
*   **Action**: Scroll to Sustainability Metrics.
*   **Narrative**: "By intercepting this event, we've just prevented the loss of 22kg of annual carbon absorption. Today, VanRakshak-X has preserved ₹1.1 Lakhs in environmental capital. This is not just a sensor—it is environmental intelligence in action."
*   **Visual**: Point to the 'Carbon Prevented' and 'Preserved Value' metrics.

## Phase 6: Conclusion
*   **Narrative**: "VanRakshak-X: From reactive detection to proactive intelligence. Thank you."
