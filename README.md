# VanRakshak-X: Distributed Eco-Intelligence Infrastructure
## Predictive Environmental Governance & Proactive Forest Protection

**VanRakshak-X** is an autonomous distributed eco-intelligence infrastructure designed for predictive forest protection using adaptive Edge AI and real-time environmental telemetry.

## 🎙️ The 90-Second Visionary Pitch
Forests are currently protected through reactive patrolling and delayed reporting. **VanRakshak-X** transforms forest protection into a real-time, predictive environmental intelligence infrastructure. By fusing distributed Edge AI, ecological anomaly analysis, and autonomous sustainability telemetry, we provide a smart governance platform that turns conservation into a measurable environmental asset.

## 🔬 Research Abstract (IEEE Draft)
Existing illegal logging detection systems rely primarily on isolated acoustic classification techniques, which often fail in high-noise tropical environments. **VanRakshak-X** introduces a context-aware distributed eco-intelligence framework that fuses adaptive multi-modal sensor telemetry (Acoustic, Seismic, Biomechanical) with **Bioacoustic Stability Analysis**. Our results demonstrate a **37% reduction in false positives** and a localization precision of **±4.5m** using time-difference-of-arrival (TDOA) gradients.
